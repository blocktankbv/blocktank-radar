# 📡 BlockTank Radar

**Signals on our radar.** Projects, opportunities, and organizations worth watching — interesting enough to track, not yet a commitment.

## What goes here

Things that fit at least one of:
- **Strategic proximity** — intersects with our work but isn't a core project
- **Market signal** — indicates a space that might matter for ZHS, TMS, or future ventures
- **Potential partnership** — could become a collaborator, client, or channel
- **Dormant opportunity** — too early to act, but worth documenting before the window closes

## What doesn't go here

- Active projects (those live in their own repos)
- Dead ends (just close them)
- Pure research notes with no action angle

## Structure

Each entry lives in its own folder:

```
/signals/<name>/
  README.md         # Overview, why we're watching
  analysis.md       # Deep-dive: what we know, who the people are, opportunities
  actions.md        # Concrete next steps if/when we decide to move
  sources.md        # Links, posts, contacts (optional)
```

## Status tags

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| 🟢 Active watch | Checking in regularly |
| 🟡 On hold | Noted, revisit later |
| 🔴 Closed | Moved on, documented why |
