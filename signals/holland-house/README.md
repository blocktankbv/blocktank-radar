## What is Holland House?

Holland House Colombia is the Dutch-Colombian Chamber of Commerce, headquartered in Bogotá (Calle 90 # 19-41). They facilitate trade and investment between the Kingdom of the Netherlands and Colombia — organizing commercial missions, matchmaking events, B2B introductions, and sector studies.

**Director:** Jan Willem van Bokhoven  
**Website:** hollanhousecolombia.com  
**LinkedIn:** Active (~500+ posts/year, 6-month analysis done Mar 2026)

## Why This Is on the Radar

1. **Dual geography** — BlockTank BV is registered in Amsterdam, Juan is Colombian. We are the exact profile Holland House exists to serve.
2. **Sector alignment** — Holland House runs active programs in AI/tech, water/climate infrastructure, agrifood, floriculture, and port logistics. ZHS is positioning in the SME automation space, which crosscuts all of these.
3. **Mission operator** — They already ran a **digital tech commercial mission to the Dominican Republic** for Colombian tech companies (15 companies, 70+ meetings). A future Europe-bound tech mission is plausible.
4. **Dutch capital flows** — Dutch government (RVO, DFCD, Invest International) is actively funding Colombian projects through Holland House affiliates. Knowing the pipeline = deal flow.
