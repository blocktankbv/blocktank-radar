- June 2024: 67th ACODAL Water, Waste & Energy Congress in Cartagena — largest Dutch delegation ever. Holland House + NWP co-hosted the Netherlands Pavilion. Jan Willem keynoted.
- March 2026: RVO webinar on Cartagena water sector opportunities — 4 sub-sectors identified (see below)

## The Problem (Why Cartagena)

Cartagena is Colombia's #1 port city and a major tourism hub (UNESCO heritage, beach tourism). It has 40km+ of canals. But it's struggling with:

- Canals and coastline poorly maintained, vulnerable to flooding
- Low water quality
- Waste not properly processed → water contamination
- Climate exposure increasing

---

## Opportunity Sectors for Dutch Companies

1. **Flood management solutions**
2. **Water purification & sanitation technology**
3. **Nature-based solutions (NBS)** for coastal/urban climate resilience
4. **Pilot projects** in urban planning + sustainable infrastructure
