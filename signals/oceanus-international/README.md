## What is Oceanus International?

Coastal engineering company. Founded 2005. Operates as a full **Design-Build-Operate (DBO)** partner — they take responsibility from engineering through long-term operation, not just consulting.

**Website:** https://oceanusinternational.com  
**Beach recovery site:** https://recuperaciondeplayas.com/en/

**Core services:**
- Beach reclamation + restoration + maintenance
- Coastal erosion control
- Flood management
- Sargassum interception + processing
- Nature-based solutions (NBS)

**Proprietary tech:**
- Geocylinders® — custom dewatering for beach erosion
- AmA® Artificial Modular Reefs — 3D-printed prefab reefs
- Roboworm — small-scale marine dredging system
- Marine Gabions
- Sargassum early detection system (patented, with Caribbean bathymetric data)

**Academic partners:** Delft University of Technology, University of South Florida Coastal Research Lab, in-house PhDs. IRP Delft helped with LatAm/Caribbean expansion strategy (2017).
